Publications

Zenodo scientific publications by Professor Nona Dronova, indexed by reference object (RO-JADE-001–024)

This page indexes the open-access Zenodo publications associated with each reference object in the jade collection. Each entry below is listed under the atlas object it documents; several objects share a broader survey or methodological publication in addition to an object-specific article. All DOIs resolve to Zenodo records under the CC BY 4.0 licence, "Global Archive of Antique Porcelain Authentication" community.

The publications listed here are cited as bibliographic references for further reading. Their titles and content reflect the author's published scholarly record; inclusion on this page is not a re-statement of any authentication verdict by the atlas, and readers should consult each publication directly for its own methodology and conclusions.
